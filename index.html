Privacy Policy for BA Studio EU Applications

Last updated: 2026-06-03

This Privacy Policy applies to the following applications published by BA Studio EU:

  • 3D Filament Energy
  • WorkTime World

BA Studio EU respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our applications.

1. Information We Collect

The apps do not require account creation and do not collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

All project data, work records, prints, materials, settings, reports, and images added by users are stored locally on the user’s device.

2. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

The apps may offer optional premium features through a subscription system.

Payments are processed securely through Google Play Billing. The apps do not collect or store payment details such as credit card numbers.

Subscription status is managed using RevenueCat, which processes purchase-related information necessary to verify subscription status.

These services may process purchase history, subscription status, device identifiers, and technical information required for purchase validation.

All communication with third-party services is encrypted using HTTPS.

3. Notifications

WorkTime World may use local notifications and reminders configured by the user. Notification data remains on the user’s device.

4. PDF Reports and Exports

The applications may generate PDF reports and export files based on information entered by the user. These files are created locally on the user’s device.

5. Data Sharing

The apps do not sell user data.

Purchase-related data may be processed by Google Play (Google LLC) and RevenueCat for subscription validation and entitlement management.

6. Data Storage

All user-created content is stored locally on the device.

The developer does not operate a server that stores user data.

7. Data Deletion

Since the apps do not maintain user accounts or remote storage, uninstalling the apps removes locally stored data from the device.

8. Advertising

The apps do not display ads and do not use advertising SDKs.

9. Children’s Privacy

The apps are not directed to children under 13 years of age.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact:

b.a.studioeu@gmail.com
