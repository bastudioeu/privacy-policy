Privacy Policy for B.A.StudioEU Applications

Last updated: 2026-06-03

This Privacy Policy applies to applications published by B.A.StudioEU, including:

  • 3D Filament Energy

B.A.StudioEU respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our applications.

1. Information We Collect

Our applications do not require account creation and do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

All user-created content, including work records, project data, filament information, print history, settings, reports, images, and other application data, is stored locally on the user's device.

2. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Some applications may offer optional premium features through subscriptions or in-app purchases.

Payments are securely processed by Google Play Billing. B.A.StudioEU does not collect, receive, or store payment card information.

Subscription management and verification may be provided through RevenueCat. RevenueCat may process purchase-related information and anonymous device identifiers required for subscription validation.

All communication with third-party services is encrypted using secure HTTPS connections.

3. Notifications

WorkTime World may use local notifications and reminders configured by the user. Notification information remains on the user's device and is not transmitted to B.A.StudioEU servers.

4. PDF Reports and Data Export

Applications may generate PDF reports and export files based on information entered by the user. These files are created and stored locally on the user's device.

5. Data Sharing

B.A.StudioEU does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

Purchase-related information may be processed by Google Play (Google LLC) and RevenueCat solely for payment processing, subscription verification, and entitlement management.

6. Data Storage

All user-generated content is stored locally on the device.

B.A.StudioEU does not operate servers that store user-generated content or personal data.

7. Data Deletion

Since our applications do not use user accounts or cloud storage, uninstalling an application generally removes its locally stored data from the device.

8. Advertising

Our applications do not display advertisements and do not use advertising SDKs.

9. Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed toward children under the age of 13.

B.A.StudioEU may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.
