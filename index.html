Privacy Policy for 3D Filament Energy

Last updated: 2026-02-25

3D Filament Energy respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the application.

1. Information We Collect

The app does not require account creation and does not collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

All project data, prints, materials, and images added by users are stored locally on the user’s device.

2. In-App Purchases

The app offers optional premium features through a subscription system.

Payments are processed securely through Google Play Billing. The app does not collect or store payment details such as credit card numbers.

Subscription status is managed using RevenueCat, which processes purchase-related information necessary to verify subscription status.

These services may process purchase history and device identifiers.

All communication with third-party services is encrypted using HTTPS.

3. Data Sharing

The app does not sell user data.

Purchase-related data may be processed by Google Play (Google LLC) and RevenueCat.

4. Data Storage

All user-created content is stored locally on the device.

The developer does not operate a server that stores user data.

5. Data Deletion

Since the app does not maintain user accounts or remote storage, uninstalling the app removes locally stored data.

6. Advertising

The app does not display ads and does not use advertising SDKs.

7. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13 years of age.
